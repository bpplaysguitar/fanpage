The Damnwells

Bio

The Damnwells are an indie rock band from Brooklyn, New York. Formed in 2000, the band consists of singer/songwriter and guitarist Alex Dezen, drummer Steven Terry, guitarist David Chernis, and bassist Ted Hudson. Other studio and touring members over the years have included Andrew Ratcliffe (drums), Adrian Dickey (bass), Freddy Wall (guitar), and Jay Barclay (guitar) who also played with band Augustana. The Damnwells mostly retired in 2016, though Alex Dezen continued to release and tour with his solo career and other projects. The band does occasionally get back together to play a show for their die-hard fans, usually around once a year.
